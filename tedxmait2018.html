GLIMPSES OF TEDxMAIT-2018


MAIT organized its first-ever edition of TEDx on March 17th, 2018.

"From Ideas to Action", the theme of TEDxMAIT 2018, focused on the journey of an idea as it transforms a thought to action.

As successful as it was, through dedication and hard work of the team involved and personalities from different walks of life and fields of expertise coming and sharing their experiences and knowledge made the event a glorious one.

10 Brilliant Speakers from diverse walks of life came under one roof to showcase some exceptional success stories and thought-provoking ideas.
